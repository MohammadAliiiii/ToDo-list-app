About the ToDo-list App

The ToDo-list app is a simple and intuitive tool designed to help you manage your tasks effectively. Here's how it works:

1. Enter your task in the input box.

2. Click the "Add ToDo" button to add the task to your list.

3. Click on a task to mark it as completed (or undo the completion).

4. Click the 'x' icon next to a task to remove it from the list.

5. Your tasks are saved locally, so you can access them even if you close the browser.

Stay organized and focused with our ToDo-list app. Thank you for choosing our application!
